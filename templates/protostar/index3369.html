Tenders & Contracts

Tenders

Tendering process is being handled by the Tenders and Contracts Branch of the Board situated at our Head Office in Galle Road, Ratmalana, Sri Lanka. The branch is headed by the Assistant General Manager, Tenders and Contracts. Telephone +94 11 2605328. Our tendering processes are designed for transparency and value for money.

Current Tenders

Please click the 'down arrow' to view the tender notice. If you are interested in the tender, you can visit the Tenders and Contracts Branch at Ratmalana Head office and collect the tender documents on payment of the non refundable tender fee.

General Tender Informations 
  • Bidding documents will be issued on production of a letter of request for documents on a business letterhead together with the proof for eligibility.
  • Whenever necessary bidders should get registered in terms of the Public Contract Act No. 3 of 1987 before collecting the documents.
  • Bidding documents may be inspected free of charge at the place of issuing documents during normal working hours.
  • Bidding documents can be collected between 09.00 hours to 15.00 hours on normal working days up to day before the closing date.
  • Bids will be opened soon after closing in the presence of the bidder’s authorized representatives who choose to attend. Late bids will be returned unopened.
  • Tenderers are advised to ensure that they possess required financial and technical capabilities, as stipulated in the instructions to bidders and conditions of bid of respective bidding document

Procurement Objectives

  • To provide a system which is flexible enough to procure in a timely manner the diverse range of goods, works and services required by the Board;
  • To maintain transparency in selecting the successful contractors and suppliers;
  • To ensure that goods, works and services obtained by the Board are of appropriate quality.

Advertising

Tenders are generally advertised in the news papers (Daily News, Dinamina and Thinakaran). Closing time and address for lodgment is given in the tender notice. Tenders must be lodged in the Tender Box kept at Tenders and Contracts Branch of the Head Office of the National Water Supply & Drainage Board, 2nd Floor, Galle Road, Ratmalana not later than the closing time stated on the tender notice. Tenders will be opened in the presence of tenders at the opening time. The tender or offer must be enclosed in a sealed plain envelope bearing:

  • the tenderer's name and postal address;
  • the Tender Number; and
  • the description of the Tender.

A tender will not be accepted if delivered by telephone, facsimile or electronic means of delivery. Late tenders will not be accepted.

Conditions of Tendering

The Tender must comply in all respects with the Tender Conditions Stipulated in the Tender Document.
